# Mongolbank Policy Targets: Inflation vs Exchange Rate

## Bottom line

Mongolbank operates an **inflation-targeting** framework and does **not** publicly state a fixed USD/MNT target level in the way a peg or crawling band regime would.

## What is explicit

- Official monetary-policy materials state that Mongolbank's intermediate objective is price stability.
- Parliament and Mongolbank policy materials refer to lowering the inflation target to **5 percent from 2027**.
- Mongolbank descriptions of the FX regime characterize it as a **flexible exchange-rate regime**, with the exchange rate responding to market conditions.

## Practical interpretation for this project

- Use inflation targets as a formal policy anchor.
- Treat USD/MNT as a managed-but-not-explicitly-targeted outcome variable.
- For the blog post, it is safer to say:
  - Mongolbank targets inflation.
  - Mongolia does not appear to publish a formal USD/MNT target path as a standing policy target.
  - Government budget and macro-framework documents may still contain working exchange-rate assumptions for planning scenarios.
